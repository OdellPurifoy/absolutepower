About Us

Located in Williamsburg Brooklyn, Absolute Power is a privately owned gym, fully equipped with a variety of machines, free weights and cardio equipment.

We offer a friendly, accommodating, and motivating atmosphere that allows our members to get the most out of their workouts. Please take a look at our membership packages or stop in for a tour. We look forward to seeing you!
